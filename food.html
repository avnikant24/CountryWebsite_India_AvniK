India is known for its incredible food; filled with aromatics and spices. The flavors are beyond comprehensible and are very powerful. Many curries are made from spices like tumeric and cumin, which are normally used. India has a wide variety of sweets as well.

Some popular dishes include samosas, which are fried or baked pastries with a savory filling, and dosa, a type of thin, crispy pancake made from fermented rice and lentil batter. Indian cuisine also features a variety of breads like naan and roti, often served alongside flavorful curries such as butter chicken and chana masala. Additionally, India is famous for its diverse range of sweets, including gulab jamun (deep-fried dough balls soaked in syrup) and jalebi (sweet, spiral-shaped fried batter soaked in sugar syrup).
