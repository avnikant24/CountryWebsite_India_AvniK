Holi, one of the most famously-known traditions of India that is the festival of colors. It celebrates spring's arrival and highlights the triumph of good over evil.

Indian dances like kathak and bharatnatyam are performed during festivals and cultural events. They often have deep meanings that evoke spirtuality and deep feelings in a poetic and calm manner.

India has many traditions that define its unique cultural heritage.

These traditions range from well-known festivals like Diwali and Holi to ancient practices and greetings, to basic common respect for your elders.

Some of these festivals draw international attention and admiration like Holi and Diwali!

In this webpage, you can explore some common traditions of Indian culture like clothing, designing, art, festivals, and dance.

Traditional Indian clothing like sarees, kurtas, salwari, and more, are worn during festivals and special occasions, showcasing the rich cultural heritage of India.

Henna or mehendi, is a common Indian-practice for women to apply intricate designs on their hands and feet before or during weddings and festivals.
